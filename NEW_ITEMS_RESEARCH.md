# Research for 3 New Items - Minecraft Bedrock Edition

## 1. minecraft:bowl

### Research from minecraft.wiki:
- **Item ID**: minecraft:bowl
- **Category**: Crafting material (materials/crafting.js)
- **Obtaining**: 
  - Crafted from 3 wooden planks (any type) in a V shape
  - Dropped by adult turtles when killed by lightning
  - Can be caught while fishing (junk category)
- **Usage**: 
  - Crafting stews: Mushroom Stew, Rabbit Stew, Beetroot Soup, Suspicious Stew
  - Returned after eating stews
- **Max Stack**: 64
- **Renewable**: Yes (wood, fishing, turtle drops)

## 2. minecraft:sugar_cane

### Research from minecraft.wiki:
- **Item ID**: minecraft:sugar_cane
- **Category**: Crafting material/plant (materials/crafting.js)
- **Obtaining**: 
  - Grows naturally near water in Overworld (Y=63, both dirt/sand)
  - Found in desert village chests
  - Can be farmed on dirt, sand, podzol, or mycelium next to water
- **Usage**: 
  - Crafted into sugar (1:1 ratio)
  - Crafted into paper (3 sugar cane in horizontal row)
- **Max Stack**: 64
- **Renewable**: Yes (farmable)
- **Special Properties**: 
  - Grows 1 block every 18 minutes (random ticks)
  - Can grow up to 3 blocks tall naturally
  - Can be bone-mealed to grow taller

## 3. minecraft:poisonous_potato

### Research from minecraft.wiki:
- **Item ID**: minecraft:poisonous_potato
- **Category**: Food (consumables/food_raw.js)
- **Obtaining**: 
  - 2% drop chance when harvesting potato crops
  - Not craftable or smeltable
- **Food Effects**: 
  - Restores 2 hunger points (1 drumstick)
  - Restores 1.2 saturation
  - 60% chance to inflict Poison II for 4 seconds (2.5 hearts damage)
- **Max Stack**: 64
- **Usage**: 
  - Emergency food only (high risk)
  - Can be composted (85% success rate)
  - Cannot be used in any crafting recipes
- **Rarity**: Common (low drop rate from common crop)
